About Us

Who we are

We are a dedicated team of animal lovers who have come together to create a safe and comfortable environment for cats in need.

Our mission

Our mission is to provide a temporary home for cats that are abandoned, abused, or neglected, and to help them find their forever homes through adoption events and partnerships with local pet stores and rescue organizations.

Get involved

If you share our passion for animal welfare, we invite you to volunteer at our shelter, donate supplies or funds, or adopt a cat in need.
